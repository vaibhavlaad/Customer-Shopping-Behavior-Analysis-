# Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

# Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behavior using data analytics and visualization techniques. The objective is to identify purchasing trends, customer preferences, revenue drivers, and business growth opportunities through interactive dashboards and data-driven insights.

# Project Features

* Customer Demographics Analysis
* Revenue & Sales Performance Analysis
* Product Category Performance Evaluation
* Subscription & Customer Retention Insights
* Purchase Frequency Analysis
* Payment Method & Shipping Preference Analysis
* Interactive Dashboard Visualization
* Business Recommendations and Growth Strategies

# Tools & Technologies Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Jupyter Notebook
* Power BI
* Data Visualization

# Dataset Information

* Total Records: 3,900 Customers
* Attributes:

  * Age
  * Gender
  * Product Category
  * Purchase Amount
  * Subscription Status
  * Payment Method
  * Shipping Type
  * Review Ratings
  * Purchase Frequency
  * Discount Usage

# Key Insights

* Total Revenue Generated: $233,081
* Average Purchase Amount: $59.76
* Clothing is the highest revenue-generating category.
* Clothing and Accessories contribute approximately 77% of total revenue.
* Male customers represent around 68% of the customer base.
* Only 27% of customers are subscribed to the membership program.
* Customer demand remains relatively stable across all seasons.

# Business Recommendations

1. Improve subscription adoption through loyalty programs and exclusive offers.
2. Expand high-performing categories such as Clothing and Accessories.
3. Increase Outerwear sales using targeted promotions and product bundles.
4. Implement personalized marketing campaigns based on customer demographics and purchase history.
5. Leverage customer reviews to improve customer satisfaction and conversion rates.

# Dashboard Preview

The interactive dashboard provides:

* Revenue Analysis
* Customer Segmentation
* Category Performance
* Subscription Insights
* Purchase Trends
* Business Recommendations

# Project Outcome

This project demonstrates the application of data analytics and business intelligence techniques to transform raw customer data into actionable business insights, supporting data-driven decision-making and business growth.
